A beautiful time zone clock app that shows real-time local time for 463 cities around the world.

KEY FEATURES
• World Clock: Add your favorite cities and view their local times at a glance
• Time Difference Calculator: Instantly calculate time differences between two cities
• Intuitive UI: Dark theme design that's easy on your eyes

PERFECT FOR
• Staying in touch with friends and family abroad
• Frequent international travelers
• International business professionals
• Checking times for overseas events and broadcasts

COVERAGE
Supports 463 major cities across Asia, Europe, North America, South America, Africa, and Oceania.

HIGHLIGHTS
• Completely free with no ads
• Works offline
• Privacy-focused (no data collection)
